# THE QUESTIONARRIE TO FILTER USERS AT THE START

Intro text before questions:

> "Lumora is designed for mild to moderate stress, anxiety, and OCD-related challenges.
> If you are experiencing severe distress or crisis, professional support may be more appropriate."

Use a simple 4-point scale:

- 0 = Not at all
- 1 = Occasionally
- 2 = Often
- 3 = Almost every day

---

## Section A – Emotional Severity

01. In the past 2 weeks, how often have you felt extremely hopeless or overwhelmed?
02. In the past 2 weeks, how often have your emotions felt out of control?
03. Have your thoughts caused you severe distress that feels unbearable?

---

## Section B – Daily Functioning

04. Has your mental health stopped you from attending school/work regularly?
05. Are you unable to complete basic daily tasks (eating, hygiene, sleeping)?
06. Have your symptoms caused serious problems in relationships?

---

## Section C – OCD / Anxiety Intensity

07. Do you spend more than 1 hour per day stuck in intrusive thoughts or compulsions?
08. Do you feel unable to resist compulsions at all?
09. Does anxiety frequently cause panic attacks or physical shutdown?

---

## Section D – Risk Screening (Critical)

These questions should be YES/NO.

10. Have you recently had thoughts of harming yourself?
11. Have you recently felt that life is not worth living?
12. Have you ever made a recent plan to hurt yourself?
13. Are you currently in a situation where you feel unsafe?

---

## Section E – Support & Stability

14. Do you currently have access to professional mental health support?
15. Do you have at least one trusted person you can talk to?

---

## How to Filter Users

You can use a simple logic structure:

### Immediate Professional Help Recommended If:

- Any YES to questions 10–13
- OR Extremely high total score in Section A & B
- OR Severe daily functioning impairment

### If High Risk → Show This Message

> "Based on your responses, Lumora may not be the right level of support for you at this time.
> We strongly encourage you to seek professional help.
> You deserve proper care and support."

Then provide:

- Crisis hotline (based on country)
- Emergency services
- Therapist directory link
- Option to exit app

### If Moderate / Mild → Allow Access

Show:

> "Lumora can support you alongside your journey. Remember, this app does not replace professional care."

---

## Important Ethical Notes

As the app creator I

- Always include disclaimer: "Not a substitute for therapy"
- Include emergency help button inside app
- Never rely only on this questionnaire for crisis detection
- Re-screen periodically (e.g., every 30 days)
